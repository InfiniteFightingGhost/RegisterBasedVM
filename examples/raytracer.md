# 3D Raytracer Visual Render Example

This example highlights the VM's ability to execute complex graphics workloads by rendering a 3D scene with dynamic camera movements.

---

## 1. High-Level Description

The raytracer program renders a scene containing two spheres:
- **Red Floating Sphere:** Positioned at Center $(0, 0, 3)$ with Radius $1.0$.
- **Green Ground Sphere:** Positioned at Center $(0, -100.5, 3)$ with Radius $100.0$.

The raytracer calculates per-pixel diffuse Lambertian shading and ambient reflection, adding a dynamic gradient sky background. To avoid color banding in the sky, it applies a random grain noise using the fast PRNG.

---

## 2. Dynamic Camera Orbit

The camera orbits the scene at a radius of $3.5$ around the center red sphere. The frame generation loop modifies the trigonometric camera parameters frame-by-frame:
- **Orbital Angle $\theta$:** Rotates through $360^\circ$ across 30 distinct frames.
- **Camera Coordinates ($O$):** Derived dynamically and injected into the VM's constant pool before execution.
- **Output:** Rendered as individual PPM image files, merged into a single loop animation.
